The EU Deforestation Regulation requires structured due diligence—not third-party certificates alone.

## Key dates

- **30 December 2024** — applicable for large operators and non-SME traders
- **30 June 2025** — extended deadline for micro and small enterprises

## What to organize now

1. Catalog products in scope (cocoa, coffee, palm, soy, rubber, wood, cattle)
2. Supplier surveys with geolocation where required
3. Record DDS numbers and batch validity
