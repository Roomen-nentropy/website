Who this is for

Regulation (EU) 2025/40 requires clear packaging information—materials, recyclability, and often batch-level QR transparency. Nentropy links label design, batch printing, and conformity declarations.

What makes daily work harder

  • Material composition and recyclability on labels
  • Batch QR pages for consumers and audits
  • Conformity declarations and supplier documents

How Nentropy helps

Label designer

Templates with materials, recyclability, and required fields.

Batch labelling

Generate PDF/PNG labels and trace tokens.

Public trace page

QR to /trace/:token with no consumer login.

2025/40 self-declarations

Template-driven packaging conformity documents.

Survey block

Suppliers upload technical specs and conformity files.

Product linkage

From product grids and own goods to labelling.
